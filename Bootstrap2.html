Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport played either as singles (one vs. one) or doubles (two vs. two) that involves hitting a feathered or plastic shuttlecock (also called a birdie) over a net. It is a high-energy game requiring quick reflexes and, unlike tennis, 
the shuttlecock must be kept in the air without touching the ground.
The best age to start badminton training generally falls between 5 and 8 years old A player should not reach over the net to hit the shuttlecock. The first side to 21 points wins a game. A point is scored on every serve and awarded to whichever side wins the rally.
The winning side gets the next serve. If the score is 20-20, a side must win by two clear points to win the game.
